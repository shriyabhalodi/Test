About US

Established in 1996, Digitron System, Inc. has been a trusted professional services provider in the Delaware Valley region. As a premier technology integration consulting firm, we cater to businesses of all sizes, offering tailored solutions to meet their diverse needs. Our overarching mission is to deliver high-quality personnel and effective solutions that align with our clients' short and long-term objectives.

With over sixty years of combined technology expertise, we offer specialized solutions in video entertainment, internet connectivity, and VoIP phone systems. From turnkey setups to seamless implementation and ongoing support, we excel in central computer room installations, network configurations, and hardware/software selection, ensuring timely and budget-friendly project delivery.

Conveniently located east of King of Prussia, our proximity to Metropolitan Philadelphia and its neighboring suburbs ensures accessibility and ease of collaboration. With Digitron System, Inc. by your side, you can confidently concentrate on your core business operations, knowing that your technology needs are in capable hands.
